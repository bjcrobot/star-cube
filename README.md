# STAR CUBE

## Introduction

In the Cosmic Era, year 4227, we successfully located a bizarre celestial body that defies the established laws of physics, utilizing the newly developed observation technology: the R.T.P. System (Runic Tetra-Pixilation).

To our astonishment, its fundamental structure is composed of cubes. This fundamentally overturns the long-standing axiom of astrophysics—that celestial bodies inevitably converge into spheres due to gravity. It is, in every sense, a paradigm shift.

Should our observations prove accurate, this discovery will render every existing cosmic thesis obsolete, demanding an immediate and total revision of our understanding of the universe.

Even more inscrutable is the behavior of this anomalous entity. Despite eternally fragmenting, it appears to possess a will of its own, autonomously reconfiguring its shape toward a "correct" state.

Consequently, we have designated this planetary survey project "STAR CUBE" and commenced individual observation activities. We shall continue our investigation in close coordination with relevant institutions.

Does it truly exist, or is it merely a phantom within our vision?

https://star-cube.net/

May the Cube be with you.

## Overview

This repository is an implementation testbed for a cube puzzle.
Planned as a trilogy; Magic Cube is the first release.
Just showing a cube felt too plain, so we staged it as a planet in space instead.
Click the cube in the portal screen to warp to the Magic Cube planet.
Built purely with front-end web tech, it runs locally and can be installed as a PWA.


## Tech Stack

- three.js
- JavaScript
- HTML/CSS

## How to Run
---
1. Run `test_server.py` in an environment with Python installed.
2. Open `http://localhost:8000` in your browser.


## PWA

- Bundles `manifest.webmanifest` and `sw.js`.
- Access over HTTPS (or localhost).
- Icons: `assets/images/icon-192.png`, `assets/images/favicon.png`
- Screenshots: `assets/images/pwa-screenshot.png`, `assets/images/pwa-screenshot-mobile.png`


## Controls

Top screen
- Click: Start warp to the cube
- `M`: Toggle Music
- `A`: Toggle About

magic-cube screen
- Top-right star: Return to top
- `M`: Toggle Music
- `S`: Toggle Sound
- `R`: Reset
- `ArrowLeft/Right`: Adjust Speed
- `ArrowUp/Down`: Toggle HUD


## Notes
